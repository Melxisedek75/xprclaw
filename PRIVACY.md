# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-23_

GCSC CLAW ("**we**", "**us**") operates the landing page at `melxisedek75.github.io/xprclaw` and the GCSC CLAW Progressive Web App (the "**App**"). This policy explains what information we handle and how.

## 1. What we collect

**Landing page (this site):**
- Anonymous, aggregated traffic statistics (page views, referrer, country, device class) via a privacy-friendly analytics provider (Plausible or equivalent). No cookies, no cross-site tracking, no personal identifiers.
- If you submit your email to the **waitlist form**, we store that email and the submission timestamp solely to contact you about private-beta access and major product updates.

**App (xprclaw PWA):**
- The App runs entirely in your browser's security sandbox. It does not collect your files, contacts, or device identifiers.
- When you **connect a wallet**, we process only the public XPR account name and public keys you explicitly authorise. We never ask for, handle, or store private keys or seed phrases.
- Transactions you submit are broadcast directly from your wallet to the XPR Network and recorded on a public blockchain — we are not an intermediary.

## 2. What we do **not** do

- We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.
- We do **not** use third-party tracking cookies.
- We do **not** require you to create an account to install or use the App.
- We do **not** have custody of your funds at any point.

## 3. Service providers

The following processors may receive limited data strictly to operate the service:

| Provider | Purpose | Data |
|---|---|---|
| GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc.) | Hosting of this landing page | Standard web server logs |
| Plausible / GTM (configurable) | Aggregate analytics | Anonymised traffic data |
| Mailchimp / ConvertKit (configurable) | Waitlist email delivery | Email you submit, submission timestamp |
| XPR Network validators | Blockchain execution | On-chain transactions you sign |

You can remove yourself from the waitlist at any time by replying "unsubscribe" to any email we send.

## 4. Your rights

Under GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA you have the right to access, correct, export, or delete the personal data we hold about you (essentially: your waitlist email). Send a request to **gcscdao@gmail.com** and we will action it within 30 days.

## 5. Security

- All network traffic to this site is served over HTTPS (TLS).
- We do not store passwords, since we do not operate user accounts.
- Wallet operations rely on cryptographic signatures produced locally in your browser and never leave your device unencrypted.

## 6. Trading Bot disclosure

The optional Python **Trading Bot** backend (bundled in the source ZIP for self-hosting) connects to third-party exchanges using API keys **you** provide and store locally on **your** machine. We never receive those keys.

## 7. Children

The App and this site are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 8. Changes

We may update this policy to reflect legal, technical, or business changes. The "Last updated" date at the top will change. Material changes will be announced on the landing page.
