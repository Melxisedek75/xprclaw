# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 2026**

## Overview

GCSC CLAW ("we", "us", "our", or "the Service") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Data Collection

**We collect minimal data:**
- Email address (via waitlist form, optional)
- Usage analytics via Plausible (privacy-friendly, no cookies, no tracking pixels)
- Device information (browser, OS) only for analytics

**We do NOT collect:**
- Passwords (app is client-side only)
- Private keys or wallet data (stored locally on your device)
- Location data
- Browsing history

## Analytics

We use **Plausible Analytics**, a GDPR-compliant, privacy-first service:
- No cookies or personal identification
- No third-party trackers
- Data is aggregated and anonymized

## Storage

- **Waitlist emails**: Stored on Formspree servers (email service)
- **App data**: Stored locally on your device
- **Wallet credentials**: Never leave your device
